[60] (empty)
[61] FGSM Attack Configuration
[62] FGSM attacks were executed at inference time with ε values of 0.01, 0.03, 0.05, and 0.10 on both the baseline and robust models.
[63] These epsilon values were chosen to test weak, medium, and stronger attacks while keeping lower values hard to see by eye.
[64] Adversarial Training Configuration
[65] The robust model was trained for 10 epochs with FGSM at ε = 0.03 during learning, using combined clean and adversarial cross-entropy loss with Adam at lr = 0.001.
